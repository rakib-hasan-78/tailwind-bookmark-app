Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

what is bookmark ?

A bookmark is a thin marking tool, commonly made of card, leather, or fabric, used to keep track of a reader's progress in a book and allow the reader to easily return to where the previous reading session ended.

How can I request a bookmark for the new browser ?

Requesting a new bookmark is simple! Follow these steps to add a new bookmark to your browser using our service: Log in to Your Account: Open our website and log in to your account using your credentials. Access the Bookmark Manager: Navigate to the dashboard or the bookmark manager section of our site. Add a New Bookmark: Look for an "Add Bookmark" button or a "+" icon. Click on it. Enter the URL of the website you want to bookmark. Provide a name and description (optional) for easy identification. Categorize and Save: Choose a category or folder for the bookmark if you have organized your bookmarks into folders. Click "Save" to add the bookmark to your list. Alternatively, if you're using our browser extension: Open the Browser Extension: Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar. Add Bookmark Through Extension: Click on "Add Bookmark." The extension will automatically capture the current page URL. You can edit the name and description if needed. Select a category or folder (optional). Click "Save."

Is there a mobile app ?

Yes, we offer a mobile app for both iOS and Android devices to help you manage your bookmarks on the go! With our mobile app, you can: Easily Access Bookmarks: Quickly access all your saved bookmarks from anywhere. Add New Bookmarks: Save new websites directly from your mobile browser to our service. Organize and Edit: Categorize, rename, and delete bookmarks just like you would on the desktop version. Sync Across Devices: Enjoy seamless synchronization of your bookmarks across all your devices, ensuring you have the latest updates no matter where you are. How to Get the App: For iOS Users: Visit the App Store and search for "Our Bookmark Service" to download and install the app. For Android Users: Visit the Google Play Store and search for "Our Bookmark Service" to download and install the app.
